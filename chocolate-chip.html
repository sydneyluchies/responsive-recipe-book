  • INGREDIENTS

    1. 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, room temperature
    2. 3⁄4 cup granulated sugar
    3. 3⁄4 cup dark brown sugar
    4. 1 tsp corn syrup
    5. 2 eggs, room temperature 1 tsp vanilla extract
    6. 2 cups all-purpose flour
    7. 2 tsp Kosher salt
    8. 3⁄4 tsp baking soda
    9. 1 8 oz semisweet chocolate bar, hand chopped
    10. Coarse sea salt

    • CHOCOLATE CHIP COOKIES

      Say goodbye to chocolate chip cookies that turn out like hockey pucks

      This recipe is chewy and crispy with tons of avour. Great for an ice cream sandwich, dunking in milk or just on their own!

      PREPARATION

      In a medium bowl, whisk together the our, salt and baking powder, set aside.

      In the bowl of a stand mixer tted with the paddle attachment, cream the butter and sugars together until doubled in volume and lighter in colour, 8-10 minutes. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing the rst one in entirely before adding the second. Add the corn syrup and vanilla, mix until incorporated.

      Mix in the prepared our mixture and chopped chocolate, mix until incorporated. Using a 1 oz scoop, scoop cookie dough onto a parchment lined baking sheet, cover with plastic wrap and chill overnight.

      When ready to bake, Preheat oven to 325o. Prepare 3 rimmed cookie sheets with parch- ment paper. Place cookies on baking sheet at least 3” apart and sprinkle tops with a small amount of coarse sea salt. Transfer cookie sheets to oven and bake until golden brown around the edges, 12-15 minutes, rotating pans halfway through baking.

      Cool for 5 minutes of baking sheet and then transfer to wire rack to cool entirely. Store in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days, or as long as they last!
